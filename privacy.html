Privacy Policy

Welcome to flowhouse. We are a luxury reformer pilates and barre studio located in Rehoboth Beach, Delaware, and we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how flowhouse ("flowhouse," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website at flowhouserb.com, join our Founding Circle mailing list, create an account or make a booking through our platform, or communicate with us.

Please read this policy carefully. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described below. If you have questions or concerns, you can reach us at info@flowhouserb.com.

Note: This policy is not a substitute for the liability waiver and membership agreement you will sign at your first visit or upon enrolling as a member.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you interact with flowhouse — whether by joining our Founding Circle waitlist, creating a booking account, purchasing a membership or class package, or contacting us — you may provide us with:

  • Contact information: your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Account and booking information: username, password, class reservations, membership type, and attendance history, processed through our booking and CRM platform, Mariana Tek.
  • Payment information: billing address and payment card details. Payment transactions are processed by our payment processor (such as Stripe) and Mariana Tek. flowhouse does not store full payment card numbers on our own systems.
  • Health and fitness information: any injuries, physical limitations, pregnancy status, or physician recommendations you voluntarily disclose to us for the purpose of safe class participation.
  • Communications: the content of emails, messages, or inquiries you send us.
  • Marketing consent: your consent to receive marketing emails or SMS messages from us.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Log and device data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our website, remember your preferences, and analyze traffic. See Section 7 (Cookies & Analytics) for more detail.
  • Form submission data: if you submit your email address through a form on our website (powered by Netlify Forms), we receive and store that submission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.
  • Create and manage your account and process bookings, memberships, and payments through Mariana Tek.
  • Send you transactional and account-related communications, such as booking confirmations, class reminders, waitlist notifications, and account updates.
  • Send you marketing emails and promotional content about flowhouse, where you have given us consent to do so.
  • Send you SMS/text messages related to your account, bookings, or marketing communications, where you have separately opted in.
  • Respond to your questions, feedback, and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.
  • Protect the safety and security of our studio, staff, and clients.
  • Analyze website usage trends to improve our content and user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

flowhouse does not sell your personal information. We share your information only as described below.

Service Providers and Third-Party Processors

We work with trusted third-party service providers who process personal information on our behalf. These providers are contractually required to use your data only to perform services for us and to maintain appropriate security. Our key service providers include:

  • Mariana Tek — our booking, scheduling, and CRM platform. Mariana Tek processes your account, booking, payment, and attendance data to power class reservations and membership management. See Mariana Tek's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Netlify — our website hosting and form-processing provider. Netlify receives and temporarily stores form submissions made on our website. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Twilio and SMS providers — if you opt in to SMS communications, your phone number and messaging activity are processed by Twilio and/or related SMS service providers to deliver text messages. Your mobile information (including your phone number and consent status) will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. See Section 8 (SMS/Text Messaging) for full details.
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) — payment card information is processed directly by our payment processor. We receive transaction confirmation and billing details, but do not store raw card data.
  • Email marketing tools — we may use an email service provider to manage and send marketing emails. These providers process email addresses and engagement data (opens, clicks) on our behalf.
  • Analytics providers — we use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar) to understand how visitors use our website. These tools may use cookies and collect anonymized or aggregated data. See Section 7 for more detail.

Legal Requirements and Protection

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of flowhouse, our clients, or others.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Marketing Email Consent and CAN-SPAM Compliance

When you join our Founding Circle or otherwise opt in to email marketing, you consent to receiving promotional emails from flowhouse. Every marketing email we send will include:

  • A clear identification that the message is from flowhouse.
  • Our valid physical mailing address.
  • A clear and easy way to unsubscribe (opt out) from future marketing emails.

To unsubscribe from marketing emails, click the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email we send, or email us at info@flowhouserb.com with the subject line "Unsubscribe." We will process your request promptly and within 10 business days at the latest. Please note that even after unsubscribing from marketing emails, you may still receive transactional messages related to your account or bookings.

5. SMS / Text Messaging Consent

If you opt in to receive SMS or text messages from flowhouse, please see our SMS/Text Messaging Terms for full disclosures, including how to opt out, message frequency, and carrier disclaimers. Your mobile number and SMS consent data are not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: required for core website functionality, such as form sessions and security.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how visitors navigate and use our website so we can improve it. We may use tools such as Google Analytics. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On.
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings and preferences across visits.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. We do not currently respond to browser "Do Not Track" signals, but we will re-evaluate this as standards evolve.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, and comply with our legal obligations. Generally:

  • Account and booking information is retained for the duration of your active relationship with flowhouse, and for a reasonable period thereafter as needed for legal, tax, or business purposes.
  • Marketing consent records and opt-out records are retained to document your preferences and comply with applicable law.
  • Website analytics data is retained in accordance with the applicable analytics provider's retention settings.
  • When personal information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it in a secure manner.

8. Your Rights and Choices

General Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal retention obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing: unsubscribe from marketing emails or SMS at any time (see Sections 4 and 5).
  • Portability: request that we provide your data in a commonly used, machine-readable format where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@flowhouserb.com. We will respond to your request within 45 days. In some cases, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

California Residents — Your Choices (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and share.
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to exceptions.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. flowhouse does not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at info@flowhouserb.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will respond within 45 calendar days.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our membership and class services are intended for individuals who are at least 16 years of age; minors between 16 and 18 may participate only with parental or guardian consent as required by applicable law. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@flowhouserb.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include secure HTTPS connections on our website, access controls for our systems, and reliance on service providers who maintain industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services (for example, Mariana Tek's booking portal). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes, we will provide notice on our website or by email. Your continued use of our services after the effective date of any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@flowhouserb.com
  • Website: flowhouserb.com
  • Location: Rehoboth Beach, Delaware, USA
