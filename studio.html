what's inside

Designed for the body — and for the room you walk into.

We sourced our reformers, designed the studio, and chose every material independently. The room you train in shapes how you feel about training.

Custom equipment

Custom Flowformer machines

Our signature reformer — designed in-house and manufactured to our exact specifications. Built for the way we teach.

Two barre walls

Two dedicated barre walls

Solid wood barres on minimal black hardware — one wall for our Barre 45 classes (capped at 25), one for warmups and stretch.

Warm, intentional

Warm walnut interiors

Acoustic walnut feature walls, large sphere pendant lighting, and a calm coastal palette — designed to slow you down the moment you walk in.

the build

our atmosphere

flowhouse is a sanctuary first. The community we build inside it is a happy second. We protect the calm — and we trust the work to follow.
