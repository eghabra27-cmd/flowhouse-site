the studio · a movement sanctuary

One room. Built as a sanctuary.

flowhouse is a single, carefully designed studio on the Delaware coast. Every material was chosen — every angle was intentional — so the room feels less like a fitness studio and more like the hour you give yourself every day.

what's inside

Designed for the body — and for the room you walk into.

Our custom reformers, the studio, and every material were considered intentionally. The room you train in shapes how you feel about training.

Custom equipment

Custom FlowFormer reformers

Ten custom reformers, suited to the way we teach. Matte-black frames with the flowhouse wordmark on the side rail — the foundation of the Flowhouse Method.

Two barre walls

Two barre walls

Solid wood barres on minimal black hardware — one wall for our Barre 45 classes (twenty-five spots), one for warmups and stretch.

Warm, intentional

Warm walnut interiors

Acoustic walnut feature walls, large sphere pendant lighting, and a calm coastal palette — designed to slow you down the moment you walk in.

our atmosphere

A room built for ritual, not routine.

flowhouse is a sanctuary first. The community we build inside it is a happy second. We protect the calm — and we trust the work to follow.
