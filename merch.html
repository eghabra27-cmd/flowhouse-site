the line

A wellness line, not a logo line.

Minimal branding. Premium fabrics. Considered fits. The flowhouse line is a deliberate counter-statement to graphic-tee gym wear — designed to live in, layer with the rest of your wardrobe, and feel like the studio itself.

the first drop

A capsule, coming into focus.

Cream, taupe, walnut, oxblood — the colours of the studio itself. Movement sets, pilates grip socks, washed caps, soft tees, studio wraps, and the striped tote. Revealed piece by piece. Founding Circle sees it first.
