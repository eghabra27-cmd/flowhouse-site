the line

A wellness line, not a logo line.

Minimal branding. Premium fabrics. Considered fits. The flowhouse line is a deliberate counter-statement to graphic-tee gym wear — designed to live in, layer with the rest of your wardrobe, and feel like the studio itself.

Designed in the room it's made for.

Every piece is fit-tested in real classes on the Flowformer by the instructors who teach them. If it rides up in oblique work, it doesn't ship. If a seam catches the carriage, it doesn't ship. The line earns its way in.
