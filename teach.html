what we offer

A teaching environment built by people who teach.

Most studios optimize for the operator. We optimize for the instructor — because if the room behind the microphone is happy, the room in front of it is too.

Pay above market.

Base rate $55 per class plus per-head bonuses. Substitute slots paid at 1.5×. We publish the rate card so you never have to guess.

CEU stipend.

$1,200 per year toward continuing education — workshops, certifications, retreats. We want you better next year than this year.

Caps that protect you.

Ten reformers per class, full stop. No "we'll squeeze one more in." You'll know every body in the room and have the bandwidth to coach it.

Schedule built around you.

You set your availability monthly. We schedule around it. Want to teach mornings only? Great. Only Saturdays? Also great.

Free membership.

Unlimited classes for you on our schedule, plus partner perks. The way to stay sharp is to keep moving.

A real audition, not an interview.

We hire by class — not by résumé. You teach a real class to a real audience on a real reformer, and we decide together.

who we're looking for

The non-negotiables, the nice-to-haves.

We hire for craft and warmth, in that order. Certifications matter — but they're the floor, not the ceiling.

non-negotiable

The basics.

  • 500-hour comprehensive pilates certification (BASI, Polestar, Romana's, Body Arts & Science, STOTT, or equivalent)
  • Minimum 2 years teaching reformer in a group-class setting
  • Current CPR / AED certification (or willing to obtain before opening)
  • Lives within reasonable commute of Rehoboth (we don't hire visiting instructors)
  • Comfortable with hands-on adjustments and explicit verbal cueing
love to see

The nice-to-haves.

  • Barre certification (any reputable program)
  • Pre/post-natal certification
  • Background in dance, gymnastics, swimming, or rehab
  • Previous experience at a boutique (not corporate franchise)
  • A point of view about programming you can defend in conversation
