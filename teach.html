our hiring philosophy

Certification preferred. Movement obsession required.

We believe the best instructors are made — in the room, with mentorship, on real reformers, in front of real classes. We're open to movement-focused individuals at any starting point.

we love to see

A few things, in any combination.

  • A pilates, barre, dance, yoga, or rehab background
  • A current certification (BASI, Polestar, STOTT, Romana's, Body Arts & Science, or equivalent)
  • Group-class teaching experience — any modality
  • Comfort with hands-on adjustments and clear verbal cueing
  • A point of view about programming you can defend in conversation
  • Above all: a calm, deep love for the practice
we'll train you in

The Flowhouse Method.

  • In-house training on the Flowformer method and machine
  • One-on-one mentorship through your first 90 days
  • Programming workshops every quarter
  • A real audition — not a résumé interview
  • A founding-team seat in shaping the practice
what we offer

A teaching environment built by people who teach.

Most studios optimise for the operator. We optimise for the instructor — because if the room behind the microphone is calm, the room in front of it is too.

Complimentary membership

Unlimited reformer + barre classes on our schedule for the duration of your time on the team. Movement keeps you sharp.

Discounted merchandise

Generous discount on the flowhouse apparel line. Wear it to teach, live in it after.

Thoughtful compensation

Competitive compensation, mentorship, and long-term growth opportunities within an expanding studio environment.

In-house training & mentorship

A real apprenticeship in the Flowhouse Method, one-on-one mentorship through your first 90 days, programming workshops every quarter.

Growth with the studio

Founding-team members shape what flowhouse becomes — the programming, the apprenticeship pipeline, the future locations. There's room to grow with us.

A community-focused environment

Small, warm, calm. We hire for warmth and craft, in that order. We protect the room — for our members and our team.
