the modalities

Reformer. Barre. Equally essential.

An elevated movement studio blending reformer pilates, barre, and intentional strength-based movement — designed to support the body long-term through posture, alignment, mobility, core strength, and mind-body connection.

50 min · intermediate

Signature

Our signature reformer class blends modern strength-based movement with foundational principles inspired by Joseph Pilates. Through intentional movement, clients build deep core strength, pelvic-floor support, stability, endurance, and full-body control.

While still accessible to many clients, the pacing and sequencing lean slightly intermediate/advanced and are best suited for those with prior reformer experience.

55 min · all levels

Foundation

The perfect introduction to both the reformer and the Flowhouse Method. This slower-paced class focuses on foundational pilates principles, alignment, breath, control, and safely learning the reformer.

Designed to help clients build confidence, body awareness, and strong movement foundations. Suitable for all levels.

Barre 45

A modern barre class rooted in alignment, muscular endurance, posture, and low-impact strength training. Expect intentional movement, sculpt-based sequencing, deep core activation, and full-body burn designed to strengthen and support the body without high-impact stress.

start here

Four things to know before your first class.

flowhouse is built to welcome every body — first-timers, seasoned reformer practitioners, and everyone returning to movement. Here's the simple version.

  1. 1
    Begin with Intro or Foundation.

    Both are paced and cued for first-time movers. From there, Signature, Yin, and Barre 45 are all open to you.

  2. 2
    Wear something comfortable you can move in.

    Fitted or relaxed, your call. The flowhouse line will be available in studio at launch.

  3. 3
    Grip socks required.

    Available for purchase in studio. After one class, you'll own three pairs anyway.

  4. 4
    Bring water — beverages available in studio.

    Filtered water on-site. Stay after for a juice and meet the community.

a note on barre

Barre is beginner-friendly, low impact, posture-enhancing, strength-focused — and a perfect complement to your reformer practice.

Two barre walls, twenty-five spots per Barre 45 class, continuous movement, and intentional burn. It belongs alongside the reformer, not behind it.
