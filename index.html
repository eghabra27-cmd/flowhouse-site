the flowhouse difference

A studio built on three principles, not a hundred trends.

We don't shout. We don't gimmick. We build a room where you can hear your own breath and feel your own body — coached by instructors who actually know your name.

Slow on purpose

Coached, not corrected

Every class is capped at 10 reformers and one instructor. You'll get hands-on cues and form adjustments — not a microphone barking at the back row.

Form first

Strong & soft, by design

Reformer pilates and barre were built to develop deep stabilising strength without grinding your joints. We program for the long game, not the highlight reel.

Coastal

A studio that feels like Rehoboth

Warm white oak, arched windows, sea-grass views, and a soundtrack you'll actually want to flow to. Built locally, for locals — and the people who summer here.
