how it works

Three steps. From curious to hooked.

If reformer is new to you, this is the path of least resistance. Every founding member started here.

  1. 1
    Reserve a foundations class.

    A capped, slower-paced first reformer class. You'll learn setup, springs, and the four foundational positions — so your second class isn't a guessing game.

  2. 2
    Arrive ten minutes early.

    We'll check you in, set your springs, and walk you through the carriage before the room fills up. Grip socks and water are on us for your first class.

  3. 3
    Leave hooked.

    You'll know exactly which class to book next, and our intro pack lets you try every format in three weeks for $89.
