membership policies

The fine print — written plainly.

We believe in clear, calm policies. No surprises, no buried fees.

Cancellation

Memberships require 14 days' notice before the next billing date. Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to info@flowhouserb.com.

Pause

Monthly memberships may be paused for a minimum of 2 weeks and a maximum of 2 months. We ask for 7 days' advance notice. One pause is allowed every 6 months. Founding Unlimited members keep their founding rate through a pause.

Late cancel · No show

Our late-cancel window is 12 hours before class. Late cancels are $15. No-shows are $20. These are starting points only — Unlimited members may receive a small monthly grace allowance.
