start here

Four things to know before your first class.

Simple, intentional, no over-explanation. flowhouse welcomes every body — first-timers, seasoned movers, and everyone returning to their practice. You walk in calm and walk out taller than you came.

  1. 1
    Begin with Intro or Foundation.

    Both are paced and cued for first-time movers. From there, Signature, Yin, and Barre 45 are all open to you.

  2. 2
    Wear something comfortable you can move in.

    Fitted or relaxed, your call. The flowhouse line will be available in studio at launch.

  3. 3
    Grip socks required.

    Available for purchase in studio — quietly part of the kit you'll keep returning to.

  4. 4
    Bring water — beverages available in studio.

    Filtered water on-site. Stay after for a juice and meet the community.

a note on barre

Barre is beginner-friendly, low impact, posture-enhancing, strength-focused — and a perfect complement to your reformer practice.

Two barre walls, twenty-five spots per Barre 45 class, continuous movement, and intentional burn. It belongs alongside the reformer, not behind it.
