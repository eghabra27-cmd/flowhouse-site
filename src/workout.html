the flowhouse method

Where pilates meets strength.

The Flowhouse Method is contemporary Pilates, rooted in strength. We take the precision, breath, and control of classical reformer work and program it with the intent of modern strength training — slow, deliberate tempo and time under tension that builds real, lasting strength.

Nothing here is rushed and nothing is random. Each class is sequenced as one continuous, breath-led flow — low impact by design, athletic by intention. You'll leave stronger, taller, and calmer than you walked in.

what to expect

The same intention, every class.

However you like to move, every flowhouse class shares the same foundations. This is what stays constant, whichever class you choose.

01 · slow tempo

Time under tension.

Controlled, deliberate movement over speed. Long eccentrics recruit the deep stabilising muscles and build strength that lasts.

02 · breath-led flow

One continuous sequence.

Classes move as a single, breath-paced flow — from one position to the next without standing around, guided start to finish.

03 · low impact

Strong, never strained.

The reformer rewards control. Every class is low impact and joint-kind by design, yet intense through precise sequencing.

04 · calm room

Regulation as part of the work.

Warm walnut, soft light, and a deliberate cool down close every class. Nervous-system regulation isn't after the work — it's part of it.

Intro The perfect first flowhouse experience. New to flowhouse

Designed for clients who are brand new to reformer Pilates — or simply new to flowhouse. Your instructor introduces you to the FlowFormer, proper setup, and the foundational movements, breathing, and transitions that every other class is built on. You'll learn class etiquette and the overall flow of a flowhouse session at a welcoming, unhurried pace.

You'll practice the key movements hands-on and finish with a short five-to-ten minute guided Flow, so you leave confident and ready to step into Foundation or Signature.

good to know

A little flowhouse language.

A few terms you'll hear in the studio — so your first class feels familiar before you arrive.

The FlowFormer
Our custom reformer — the matte-black machine at the centre of every class, built specifically for the way we teach.
Time under tension
Moving slowly and with control so muscles work for longer through each rep. It's how we build strength without impact.
The Flow
A continuous, breath-led sequence that carries you from one movement to the next — the signature rhythm of a flowhouse class.
Springs & resistance
The reformer's springs set your resistance. Your instructor guides every change, so you're always working at the right load.
Eccentric
The lengthening phase of a movement. We slow it down on purpose — long eccentrics are where lasting strength is built.
Grip socks
Required for every class, for safety and hygiene. New to you? We have them available at the studio.
