her story

More than a studio. A place to return to.

With a background in pilates, yoga, holistic wellness, and mindful movement, Sam's vision was to create more than just a studio. She wanted to build a place people could return to — for ritual, connection, growth, and feeling good in their bodies.

Blending reformer pilates, barre, yoga, and sculpt-inspired movement, flowhouse is rooted in both strength and softness. Every detail — from the design to the movement itself — was created to feel welcoming, elevated, and intentional.

For Sam, movement has always been about more than fitness. It's about coming home to yourself.

what guides us

The principles flowhouse is built on.

Intentional

Nothing here is accidental. Every class, every material, every detail is chosen with care — designed to slow you down and bring you back to yourself.

Grounded

Strength and softness, together. We build the body for the long term — posture, alignment, mobility, and a calm, regulated nervous system.

Elevated

A space that feels less like a gym and more like a sanctuary. Warm, considered, and quietly luxurious — the hour you give yourself every day.

Community-centered

Movement is the start of the ritual, not the end. flowhouse is a place to be met, to belong, and to grow alongside people who move with intention too.
