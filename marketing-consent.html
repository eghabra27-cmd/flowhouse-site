Marketing & Communications Consent

Last updated: June 2026

When you join the flowhouse Founding Circle or otherwise provide your contact information through our website, you may be asked to consent to one or more types of communications from us. This page explains what you're agreeing to — and how to change your preferences at any time.

Email Marketing

By checking the email consent box on our sign-up form, you agree to receive marketing and promotional emails from flowhouse. These may include updates about our Summer 2026 opening, founding member offers, class announcements, event invitations, wellness content, and other news from the studio. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email we send, or by emailing us at info@flowhouserb.com. You will continue to receive transactional emails related to your account or bookings even after unsubscribing from marketing. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

SMS / Text Message Consent

By checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive text messages from flowhouse at the mobile number you provide. These messages may include Founding Circle updates, class booking confirmations, reminders, waitlist notifications, and — if you consent to marketing texts — promotional content. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message from us, or by emailing info@flowhouserb.com. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to SMS is not a condition of purchase. Your mobile information will not be shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes. For full details, see our SMS/Text Messaging Terms.

Photo & Video Release Acknowledgment

By attending classes or events at flowhouse, you acknowledge our photography and videography policy. In short: flowhouse may photograph or film studio activity and use those images and videos in our marketing, social media, website, and promotional materials. You grant flowhouse a perpetual, royalty-free license to use your likeness in these materials. If you prefer to opt out, you can do so at any time by sending a written request to info@flowhouserb.com. For full details, see the Photography & Videography section of our Studio Policies.

How to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw any of the above consents at any time. Simply:

  • Email marketing: click "unsubscribe" in any marketing email, or email info@flowhouserb.com.
  • SMS / text messages: reply STOP to any text from us, or email info@flowhouserb.com.
  • Photo/video likeness: email info@flowhouserb.com with the subject line "Likeness Opt-Out."

Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of any communications we sent before you withdrew, and it does not affect your ability to book classes or use flowhouse services.

For more information about how we handle your personal information, please read our Privacy Policy in full.
